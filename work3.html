Project Overview

This project is a Chinese-style pixel art action role-playing game (ARPG) that centers on traditional Chinese culture as its spiritual core. It blends the retro aesthetic of pixel art with the immersive exploration and real-time combat experience of ARPGs, creating an indie game that combines cultural depth, engaging gameplay, and distinctive visuals. The project centers on three core pillars: “Chinese aesthetic + pixel texture + hardcore action.” It breaks the visual limitations of traditional pixel games by deeply integrating Chinese elements like ink-wash painting aesthetics, classical patterns, and traditional attire with the granularity and retro charm of pixel art. Simultaneously prioritizing gameplay enjoyment and operability, it allows players to experience the unique allure of traditional Chinese culture while exploring an Eastern fantasy world.

Tools Used

Pixel Studio Unity Adobe Illustrator Github

Design Process

Research & Discovery

Conduct user research to understand the pain points and needs of game streaming users. Analyze competitors and identify opportunities for differentiation.

Wireframing

Create martial arts movements and distinctive costumes that best reflect historical and cultural characteristics, with a focus on adhering to design principles centered around Chinese aesthetics.

Visual Design

Employing a baroque visual style paired with a high-contrast black-and-white color scheme, the design enhances gameplay while ensuring immersive storytelling.

Project Outcomes

User Engagement

Following the release of the game demo, game views increased by 60%, with customer feedback indicating a heightened perception of luxury and uniqueness.

User Satisfaction

User satisfaction scores increased by 20%, while 90% of users reported that the app was “difficult to operate.”

Business Impact

Sales increased by 15% in the first quarter following the game's launch.
