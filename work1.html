Project Overview

The game project “Go back to square one” is a collaborative competition-based project I participated in. This ARPG, inspired by Metroidvania-style games, features a meticulously designed map that players explore throughout their journey. The experience is enriched with item collection mechanics alongside combat-focused gameplay.

The core appeal lies in the thrill of battling bosses while navigating these intricately crafted environments.

Tools Used

Procreate Pixel Studio Adobe Photoshop Unity

Design Process

Research & Discovery

Before creating character concept art, it is essential to thoroughly research the historical background of the story and carefully consider the artistic techniques to be employed in producing the game character designs. Only then can the resulting characters align with the game's thematic style.

Visual Design

Developed a minimalist visual style with a dark color scheme that enhances the music listening experience while ensuring readability and usability.
